## Introduction to LLMs
LLMs are sofisticated AI models capable of understanding and generating human language text and can handle various complex tasks, including summarizing, generating and translating text, they can even answer questions

### LLMs
* based on **deep learning** architectures
* Most commonly **transformers**
* Huge **neural networks** with lots of parameters and text data

**Note:** In this course we mainly use pre-training LLMs from HuggingFace which are already been trained for particular task
